Supermassive Black Hole TON 618

Overview

TON 618 is a supermassive black hole located in a quasar approximately 10.37 billion light-years away from Earth in the constellation Canes Venatici. It is one of the largest known black holes, with an estimated mass of around 66 billion solar masses.

Discovery

TON 618 was discovered in the early 1970s as part of a survey of quasars. Its immense size and brightness have made it a subject of interest in the study of black holes and the evolution of galaxies.

Characteristics

  • Type: Supermassive black hole
  • Mass: Approximately 66 billion solar masses
  • Distance from Earth: About 10.37 billion light-years
  • Location: Constellation Canes Venatici
